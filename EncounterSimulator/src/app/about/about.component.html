Using the encounter simulator

The encounter simulator is a tool intended for dungeon masters and players alike to track their combat encounters while playing Dungeons & Dragons.

Users can create and manage their characters before selecting characters for combat, rolling initiative, and simulating the entire encounter.

During the combat, each turn can be "recorded" by selecting an action, target, and value. Optionally, enter some flavor text for the action.

All actions and rounds are tracked and logged, and all combat encounters can be reviewed.
